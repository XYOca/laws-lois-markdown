## Coming into Force

### [^]2 S.C. 2013, c. 18

These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 35 of the *Enhancing Royal Canadian Mounted Police Accountability Act*, chapter 18 of the Statutes of Canada, 2013, comes into force but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.
