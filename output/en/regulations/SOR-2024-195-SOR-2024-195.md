> Critical Habitat of the Spotted Gar (Lepisosteus oculatus) Order


## Critical Habitat

### 1 Application

Subsection 58(1) of the *Species at Risk Act* applies to the critical habitat of the Spotted Gar (Lepisosteus oculatus) — which is identified in the recovery strategy for that species that is included in the Species at Risk Public Registry — other than the portion of that critical habitat that is in a place referred to in subsection 58(2) of that Act.

## Repeal

### 2

The *Critical Habitat of the Spotted Gar (Lepisosteus oculatus) Order*[^] is repealed.

## Coming into Force

### 3 Registration

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
