> Order Fixing the Day for the Purposes of Section 143 of the Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1


## General

### 1 Day for purposes of section 143

The day for the purposes of section 143 of the *Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1*[^] is fixed as December 1, 2018.

## Coming into Force

### [^]2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the first day on which sections 111, 139 and 140 of the *Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1*[^] are all in force, but if it is registered after that day, it comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Labour, pursuant to section 143 of the *Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1*[^], makes the annexed *Order Fixing the Day for the Purposes of Section 143 of the Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1*.
