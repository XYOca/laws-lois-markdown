> Critical Habitat of the Rocky Mountain Sculpin (Cottus sp.) Eastslope Populations Order


### 1 Application

Subsection 58(1) of the *Species at Risk Act* applies to the critical habitat of the Rocky Mountain Sculpin (Cottus sp.) Eastslope populations, which is identified in the recovery strategy for that species that is included in the Species at Risk Public Registry.

### 2 Coming into Force

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
