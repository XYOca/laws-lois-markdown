> Listed Airport Regulations


## Interpretation

### 1

In these Regulations, **Act** means the *Air Travellers Security Charge Act*.

## Prescribed Airports

### 2

The following airports are prescribed airports for the purposes of the definition **listed airport** in section 2 of the Act:

- (a) Red Deer Regional; and

- (b) Rivière-Rouge (Mont-Tremblant International).

## Coming into Force

### 3

These Regulations come into force on December 23, 2004.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to sections 2 and 84 of the *Air Travellers Security Charge Act*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Listed Airport Regulations*.
