> Transfer of Duties Order


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the Minister of Science to the Minister of Industry the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Science under Part I of the *Budget Implementation Act, 1997*[^] that were transferred to that Minister by Order in Council P.C. 2018-1013 of July 18, 2018[^].
