> Canada-Chile Free Trade Agreement Fruit and Vegetable Aggregate Quantity Limit Order


## Tariff Rate Quota

### 1

The quantity limit of goods of tariff item No. 0703.10.92 that are entitled to the Chile Tariff is, for the period set out in column 1 of item 1 of the schedule, the applicable quantity limit set out in column 2 of that item.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/98-28, s. 30

</details>

### 2

The aggregate quantity limit of goods of tariff item Nos. 0810.10.11 and 0810.10.92 that are entitled to the Chile Tariff is, for the period set out in column 1 of item 2 of the schedule, the applicable aggregate quantity limit set out in column 2 of that item.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/98-28, s. 30

</details>

## Coming into Force

### [^]3

This Order comes into force on the day on which section 51 of the *Canada-Chile Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act*, chapter 14 of the Statutes of Canada, 1997, comes into force.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 25.8[^] of the *Customs Tariff*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Canada-Chile Free Trade Agreement Fruit and Vegetable Aggregate Quantity Limit Order*.
