# Maximum Amounts for Informal Procedure Regulations

> Regulations Increasing the Maximum Amounts that Apply in Respect of Appeals under the Informal Procedure


## Short Title

### 1

These Regulations may be cited as the *Maximum Amounts for Informal Procedure Regulations*.

## Interpretation

### 2

In these Regulations, **Act** means the *Tax Court of Canada Act*. (*Loi*)

## Maximum Amounts

### 3

The amount of $7,000 referred to in paragraph 18(1)(a), section 18.1, subsections 18.11(2) and (3) and sections 18.12 and 18.13 of the Act is increased to $12,000.

### 4

The amount of $14,000 referred to in paragraph 18(1)(b) and sections 18.1, 18.12 and 18.13 of the Act is increased to $24,000.

## Application

### 5

Sections 3 and 4 apply in respect of appeals instituted on or after September 1, 1993.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Justice, pursuant to section 18.27[^] of the *Tax Court of Canada Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations increasing the maximum amounts that apply in respect of appeals under the informal procedure*.
