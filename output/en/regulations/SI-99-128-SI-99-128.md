> Order Designating the Minister of Canadian Heritage as Minister for purposes of the Act


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to the definition **Minister** in section 2 of the *Foreign Publishers Advertising Services Act*[^], hereby designates the Minister of Canadian Heritage, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, as the Minister for the purposes of that Act.
