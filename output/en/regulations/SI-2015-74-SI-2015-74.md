> Transfer of Duties Order


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers to the Department of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness from the Department of National Defence the control and supervision of that portion of the federal public administration, known as the National Search and Rescue Secretariat.
