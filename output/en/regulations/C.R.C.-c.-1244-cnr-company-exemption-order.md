# C.N.R. Company Exemption Order

> Order Exempting the C.N.R. Company from Subsection 10(2) of the Official Languages Act


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *C.N.R. Company Exemption Order*.

## Exemption

### 2

The Canadian National Railway Company is hereby exempted from the application of subsection 10(2) of the *Official Languages Act* in respect of services provided or made available by it to the public, other than to the travelling public, at places elsewhere than in Canada.
