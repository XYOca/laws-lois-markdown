> Order Transferring from the Minister of Industry, Science and Technology to the Minister of National Health and Welfare the Powers, Duties and Functions of the Minister of Industry, Science and Technology under the Hazardous Materials Information Review Act


> Whereas, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, all the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Consumer and Corporate Affairs under any Act of Parliament were transferred to the Minister of Industry, Science and Technology by Order in Council P.C. 1993-1487 of June 25, 1993[^];
