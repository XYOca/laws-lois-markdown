> Transfer of Duties Order


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the Minister of Canadian Heritage to the Minister of La Francophonie the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Canadian Heritage under the *Official Languages Act*[^].
