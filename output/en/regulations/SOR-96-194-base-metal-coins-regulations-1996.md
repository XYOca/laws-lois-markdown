# Base Metal Coins Regulations, 1996

> Regulations Authorizing the Issue of Base Metal Coins the Composition of which Differs from the Composition Specified for those Coins in Part II of the Schedule to the Royal Canadian Mint Act


## Short Title

### 1

These Regulations may be cited as the *Base Metal Coins Regulations, 1996*.

## Issue of Coins

### 2

The issue of base metal coins the composition of which differs from the composition specified for those coins in Part II of the schedule to the *Royal Canadian Mint Act* is hereby authorized in the denomination set out in column I of an item of the schedule, of the composition set out in column II of that item, the standard weight set out in column III of that item and the margin of tolerance set out in column IV of that item.

## Coming into Force

### 3

These Regulations come into force on July 1, 1996.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Supply and Services, pursuant to paragraph 5.2(3)(a)[^] of the *Royal Canadian Mint Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations authorizing the issue of base metal coins the composition of which differs from the composition specified for those coins in Part II of the schedule to the Royal Canadian Mint Act*.
