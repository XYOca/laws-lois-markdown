> Minister of Consumer and Corporate Affairs Authority to Prescribe Fees Order


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Consumer and Corporate Affairs and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 13(b) of the *Financial Administration Act*, is pleased hereby to authorize the Minister of Consumer and Corporate Affairs to prescribe by order the fees to be paid for Contractor and Homeowners Registration Courses under the *Urea Formaldehyde Insulation Act*[^].
