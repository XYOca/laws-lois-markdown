# Regulations Prescribing Antique Firearms


## Prescription

### 1

The firearms listed in the schedule are antique firearms for the purposes of paragraph (b) of the definition **antique firearm** in subsection 84(1) of the *Criminal Code*.

## Coming into Force

### 2

These Regulations come into force on December 1, 1998.
