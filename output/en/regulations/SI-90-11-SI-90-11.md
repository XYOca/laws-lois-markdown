> Crown Waiver Order (Northwest Territories)


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to paragraph 13(a) of the *Territorial Lands Act*, is pleased hereby to order that all that portion of the lands described in the schedule hereto lying within 30.48 metres (100′) of the Ordinary High Water Mark of the Beaufort Sea in the Northwest Territories be included in the sale of the said lands.
