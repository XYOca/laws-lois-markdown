# Northern Pipeline Notice of Objection Regulations

> Regulations Prescribing the Notice of Objection Form To Be Used by a Company that Objects to an Assessment Under Section 26 of the Northern Pipeline Act


## Short Title

### 1

This Regulation may be cited as the *Northern Pipeline Notice of Objection Regulations*.

## Form

### 2

The form set out in the schedule hereto is hereby prescribed.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister responsible for the Northern Pipeline Agency[^], pursuant to subsection 26(3) of the *Northern Pipeline Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations prescribing the Notice of Objection from to be used by a Company that objects to an assessment under section 26 of the Northern Pipeline Act*[^].
