> Order Authorizing the Minister of Foreign Affairs to Prescribe the Fee


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(b)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*, hereby authorizes the Minister of Foreign Affairs to prescribe, by order, the fee to be paid by foreign nationals to participate in an international youth exchange program in Canada.
