> Critical Habitat of the Western Chorus Frog (Pseudacris triseriata) Great Lakes / St. Lawrence – Canadian Shield Population Order


### 1 Application

Subsection 58(1) of the *Species at Risk Act* applies to the critical habitat of the Western Chorus Frog (Pseudacris triseriata) Great Lakes / St. Lawrence – Canadian Shield population, which is identified in the recovery strategy for that species that is included in the Species at Risk Public Registry, other than the portions of that critical habitat that are

- (a) in a place referred to in subsection 58(2) of the Act; or

- (b) found on a reserve or any other lands that are set apart for the use and benefit of a band under the *Indian Act*.

### 2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
