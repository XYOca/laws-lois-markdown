> Regulations Specifying Territories and Indicating International Registers


### 1 Territories in Schedule 1

The territories set out in Schedule 1 are not referred to in the definition **territory of the European Union** in subsection 2(1) of the *Coasting Trade Act*.

### 2 International registers

The registers set out in Schedule 2 are international registers for the purpose of paragraph 3(2.2)(c) of the *Coasting Trade Act*.

</details>

### [^]3 Coming into force

These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 94 of the *Canada–European Union Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement Implementation Act*, chapter 6 of the Statutes of Canada, 2017, comes into force but, if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Transport, pursuant to paragraphs 7(a)[^] and (b)[^] of the *Coasting Trade Act*[^], makes the annexed *Regulations Specifying Territories and Indicating International Registers*.
