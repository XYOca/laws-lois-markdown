> Order Designating the Executive Director of the Public Appointments Commission Secretariat as Deputy Head


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(a)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*, hereby designates the Executive Director of the Public Appointments Commission Secretariat as deputy head in respect of that Secretariat.
