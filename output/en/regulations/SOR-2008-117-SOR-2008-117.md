> Designated Public Office Holder Regulations


### 1

For the purposes of paragraph (c) of the definition **designated public office holder** in subsection 2(1) of the *Lobbying Act*, the positions and classes of positions set out in the schedule are designated as positions occupied by a designated public office holder.

### 2

These Regulations come into force on July 2, 2008.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the President of the Treasury Board, pursuant to subsection 2(1)[^] and paragraph 12(c.1)[^] of the *Lobbying Act*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Designated Public Office Holder Regulations*.
