> Scott Islands Protected Marine Area Establishment Order


### 1 Establishment

There is established a protected marine area to be known as the Scott Islands Protected Marine Area in the area of the sea that forms part of the internal waters of Canada, the territorial sea of Canada and the exclusive economic zone of Canada and that is described in Schedule 1 and depicted in Schedule 2.

### [^]2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the day on which the *Scott Islands Protected Marine Area Regulations* come into force.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of the Environment, pursuant to subsection 4.1(1)[^] of the *Canada Wildlife Act*[^], makes the annexed *Scott Islands Protected Marine Area Establishment Order*.
