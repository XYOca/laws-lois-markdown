> Expiry of Section 12.2 of the Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 Regulations


### 1 Ceasing to be in force

Section 12.2 of the *Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006* ceases to be in force on October 12, 2013.

### 2 Coming into force

These Regulations are deemed to have come into force on October 13, 2013.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to paragraph 101(a) and section 102 of the *Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006*[^], makes the annexed *Expiry of Section 12.2 of the Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 Regulations*.
