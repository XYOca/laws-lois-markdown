> Expiry of the Application of Section 12.1 of the Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 Regulations


### 1

Section 12.1 of the *Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006* ceases to be in force on July 1, 2011.

### 2

These Regulations come into force on July 1, 2011.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to section 102 of the *Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006*[^] hereby makes the annexed *Expiry of the Application of Section 12.1 of the Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 Regulations*.
