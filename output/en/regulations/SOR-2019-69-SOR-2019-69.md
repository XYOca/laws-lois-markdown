> Order Extending the Application of the Freezing Assets of Corrupt Foreign Officials (Ukraine) Regulations


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs, pursuant to section 6 of the *Freezing Assets of Corrupt Foreign Officials Act*[^], extends the application of the *Freezing Assets of Corrupt Foreign Officials (Ukraine) Regulations*[^] for a period of five years beginning on March 6, 2019.
