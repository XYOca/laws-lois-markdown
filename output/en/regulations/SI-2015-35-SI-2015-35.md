> Order Designating the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development to be the Minister referred to in the Act


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 3 of the *Canadian High Arctic Research Station Act*[^], designates the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development to be the Minister referred to in that Act.
