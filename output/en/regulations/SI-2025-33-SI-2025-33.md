> Order Transferring from the President of the King’s Privy Council for Canada to the Minister of Industry the Powers, Duties and Functions in Relation to the Pacific Economic Development Agency of Canada


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, under paragraph 2‍(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the President of the King’s Privy Council for Canada to the Minister of Industry the powers, duties and functions of the President of the King’s Privy Council for Canada in relation to the Pacific Economic Development Agency of Canada.
