> Deposit Insurance Application Fee By-law


## Prescribed Fee

### 1

The prescribed fee that is to accompany an application for deposit insurance made by a provincial institution under subsection 17(1.1) of the *Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation Act* is $20,000 and is non-refundable.
