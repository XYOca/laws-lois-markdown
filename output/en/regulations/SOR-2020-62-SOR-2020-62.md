> Income Support Payment Amount Regulations


## Income Support Payment

### 1 Amount of income support payment

For the purpose of subsection 7(1) of the *Canada Emergency Response Benefit Act*, the amount of an income support payment for any week is $500.

## Coming into Force

### 2 Registration

These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> The Minister of Employment and Social Development, with the consent of the Minister of Finance, makes the annexed *Income Support Payment Amount Regulations* pursuant to subsection 7(2) of the *Canada Emergency Response Benefit Act*[^].
