> Order Giving Notice that a Tax Convention Between Canada and the United Mexican States Came into Force May 11, 1992


> Notice is hereby given, pursuant to section 18 of the *Canada-Mexico Income Tax Convention Act, 1991*[^], that the Convention between the Government of Canada and the Government of the United Mexican States For the Avoidance of Double Taxation and the Prevention of Fiscal Evasion with respect to Taxes on Income came into force on May 11, 1992.
