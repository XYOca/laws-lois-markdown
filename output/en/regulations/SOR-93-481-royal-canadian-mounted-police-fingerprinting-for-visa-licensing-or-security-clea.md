# Royal Canadian Mounted Police Fingerprinting for Visa, Licensing or Security Clearance Purposes Fee Regulations

> Regulations Prescribing the Fee for Fingerprinting for the Purposes of a Visa, Licence or Security Clearance


## Short Title

### 1

These Regulations may be cited as the *Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Fingerprinting for Visa, Licensing or Security Clearance Purposes Fee Regulations*.

## Fee

### 2

The fee to be paid by an individual for fingerprinting for the purposes of obtaining a visa, licence or security clearance is $25.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Solicitor General of Canada and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(a)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations prescribing the fee for fingerprinting for the purposes of a visa, licence or security clearance*.
