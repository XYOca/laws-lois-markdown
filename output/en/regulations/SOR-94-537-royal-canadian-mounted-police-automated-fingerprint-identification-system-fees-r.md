# Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System Fees Regulations

> Regulations Prescribing the Fees To Be Paid by the Users of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System


## Short Title

### 1

These Regulations may be cited as the *Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System Fees Regulations*.

## Fee

### 2

The fee to be paid by law enforcement agencies for the use of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System is $33.79 annually per sworn police officer.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Solicitor General of Canada and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(a)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations prescribing the fees to be paid by the users of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System*.
