> CPTPP Remission Order


### 1 Remission

Remission is granted of the customs duties paid or payable under the *Customs Tariff* in respect of goods classified under tariff item No. 4011.90.90 in the List of Tariff Provisions set out in the schedule to the *Customs Tariff* for which the CPTPT treatment is claimed and that were imported during the period commencing on December 30, 2018 and ending on the coming into force of this Order.

### 2 Condition

The remission is granted on the condition that the importer makes a claim for remission to the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness within two years after the date of importation.

### 3 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 115[^] of the *Customs Tariff*[^], makes the annexed *CPTPP Remission Order*.
