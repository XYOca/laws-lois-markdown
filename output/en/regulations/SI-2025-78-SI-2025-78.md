> Order Transferring from the Minister of Industry to the Minister of Artificial Intelligence and Digital Innovation the Powers, Duties and Functions in Relation to the Federal Economic Development Agency for Southern Ontario


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, under paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the Minister of Industry to the Minister of Artificial Intelligence and Digital Innovation the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Industry in relation to the Federal Economic Development Agency for Southern Ontario.
