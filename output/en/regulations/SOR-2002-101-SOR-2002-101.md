> Financial Consumer Agency of Canada Designated Violations Regulations


## Interpretation

### 1 Definition of Act

In these Regulations, **Act** means the *Financial Consumer Agency of Canada Act*.

## Designated Violations

### 2 Designation

The following are designated as violations that may be proceeded with under sections 20 to 31 of the Act:

- (a) the contravention of any consumer provision; and

- (b) the non-compliance with any compliance agreement entered into under an Act listed in Schedule 1 to the Act.

## Coming into Force

### 3 Coming into force

These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(a) of the *Financial Consumer Agency of Canada Act*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Financial Consumer Agency of Canada Designated Violations Regulations*.
