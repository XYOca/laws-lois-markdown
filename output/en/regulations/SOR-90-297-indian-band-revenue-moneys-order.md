# Indian Band Revenue Moneys Order

> Order Respecting the Control, Management and Expenditure of Revenue Moneys


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *Indian Band Revenue Moneys Order*.

## General

### 2

The bands listed in the schedule are hereby permitted to control, manage and expend in whole their revenue moneys.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to subsection 69(1) of the *Indian Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Order respecting the control, management and expenditure of revenue moneys*.
