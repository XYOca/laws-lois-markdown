> CUFTA Rules of Origin Regulations


## Rules of Origin

### 1 Force of law

The following provisions of the Canada–Ukraine Free Trade Agreement have the force of law in Canada:

- (a) Articles 3.1 to 3.14; and

- (b) Annexes 3-A and 3-C.

## Repeal

### 2

The *CUFTA Rules of Origin Regulations*[^] are repealed.

## Coming into Force

### [^]3 S.C. 2024, c. 3

These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 9 of the *Canada–Ukraine Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act, 2023* comes into force, but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, makes the annexed *CUFTA Rules of Origin Regulations* under subsection 16(2)[^] of the *Customs Tariff*[^].
