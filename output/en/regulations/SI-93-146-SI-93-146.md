> Order Transferring to the Department of External Affairs the Control and Supervision of the Investment Development Division in Investment Canada


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, is pleased hereby to transfer to the Department of External Affairs the control and supervision of that portion of the public service in Investment Canada known as the Investment Development Division.
