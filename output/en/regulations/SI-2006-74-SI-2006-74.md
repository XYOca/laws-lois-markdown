> Order Transferring from the Privy Council Office to the Department of Human Resources and Skills Development the Control and Supervision of the Policy Research Initiative


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, hereby transfers from the Privy Council Office to the Department of Human Resources and Skills Development the control and supervision of the portion of the federal public administration within the Privy Council Office known as the Policy Research Initiative, effective June 1, 2006.
