> Order Designating Alberta for the Purposes of the Criminal Interest Rate Provisions of the Criminal Code


## Province Designated

### 1

Alberta is designated for the purposes of section 347.1 of the *Criminal Code*.

## Coming into Force

### [^]2

This Order comes into force at 12:00 a.m. Mountain time on the first day on which section 17 of the *Payday Loans Regulation*, Alta. Reg. 157/2009, is in force.
