> Transfer of Duties Order


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers to the President of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada from the Leader of the Government in the House of Commons the powers, duties and functions of the Leader of the Government in the House of Commons under the *Canadian Transportation Accident Investigation and Safety Board Act*[^].
