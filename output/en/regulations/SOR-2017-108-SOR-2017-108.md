> Designation of Regulatory Provisions for Purposes of Enforcement (Migratory Birds Convention Act, 1994) Regulations


### 1 Designated provisions

The provisions set out in the schedule are designated for the purposes of paragraph 13(1)(c) of the *Migratory Birds Convention Act, 1994*.

### [^]2 Coming into force

These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 101 of the *Environmental Enforcement Act*, chapter 14 of the Statutes of Canada, 2009, comes into force, but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of the Environment, pursuant to paragraph 12(1)(l)[^] of the *Migratory Birds Convention Act, 1994*[^], makes the annexed *Designation of Regulatory Provisions for Purposes of Enforcement (Migratory Birds Convention Act, 1994) Regulations*.
