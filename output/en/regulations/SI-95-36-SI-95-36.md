> Order Transferring from the Minister of Employment and Immigration to the Minister of Labour, all the Powers, Duties and Functions of the Minister of Employment and Immigration under Certain Acts


> Whereas, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, all the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Labour under any Act of Parliament were transferred to the Minister of Employment and Immigration by Order in Council P.C. 1993-1488 of June 25, 1993[^];
