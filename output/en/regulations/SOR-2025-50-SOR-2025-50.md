> Special Economic Measures Permit Authorization Order


## Authorizations

### 1 Authorization

The Minister of Foreign Affairs may

- (a) issue to any person in Canada or Canadian outside Canada a permit to carry out a specified activity or transaction, or class of activity or transaction, that is restricted or prohibited under a regulation listed in the schedule; and

- (b) issue a general permit allowing any person in Canada or Canadian outside Canada to carry out a specified activity or transaction, or class of activity or transaction, that is restricted or prohibited under a regulation listed in the schedule.

### 2 Authorization — Syria

The Minister of Foreign Affairs may

- (a) issue to any person in Canada or Canadian outside Canada a permit to carry out a specified activity or transaction, or class of activity or transaction, that is restricted or prohibited under the *Special Economic Measures (Syria) Regulations* other than an activity or class of activity that is set out in section 3.4 of those Regulations; and

- (b) issue a general permit allowing any person in Canada or Canadian outside Canada to carry out a specified activity or transaction, or class of activity or transaction, that is restricted or prohibited under the *Special Economic Measures (Syria) Regulations* other than an activity or class of activity that is set out in section 3.4 of those Regulations.

## Coming into Force

### 3 Registration

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs, makes the annexed *Special Economic Measures Permit Authorization Order* under subsection 4(4)[^] of the *Special Economic Measures Act*[^].
