> Order Designating the Minister of Citizenship and Immigration, who is a federal minister, to be the Minister referred to in that Act


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 3 of the *College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants Act*[^], designates the Minister of Citizenship and Immigration, who is a federal minister, to be the Minister referred to in that Act.
