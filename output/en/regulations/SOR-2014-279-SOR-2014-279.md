> Order Declaring that the Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations Do Not Apply in Yukon


## Declaration

### 1 Definitions

For the purposes of sections 2 and 3, **effluent**, **final discharge point** and **wastewater system** have the same meanings as in section 1 of the *Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations*.

### 2 Non-application — Regulations

The *Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations*, made under subsection 36(5) of the *Fisheries Act*, do not apply in respect of a wastewater system in Yukon that would otherwise have been subject to those regulations and that is subject to the *Waters Act*, S.Y. 2003, c. 19; 2007, c. 6 and the *Waters Regulation*, Yukon O.I.C. 2003/58.

### 3 Non-application — subsection 36(3) of the Act

Subsection 36(3) of the *Fisheries Act* does not apply in respect of any deposit of effluent from the final discharge point of a wastewater system referred to in section 2 if the effluent would otherwise have been regulated under the *Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations*.

## Coming into Force

### 4 Registration

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
