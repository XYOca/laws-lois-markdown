> Order Designating the Province of Manitoba for the Purposes of the Definition “applicable guidelines” in subsection 2(1) of the Divorce Act


## Designation

### 1

The Province of Manitoba is hereby designated for the purposes of the definition **applicable guidelines** in subsection 2(1) of the *Divorce Act*.

## Guidelines

### 2

For the purposes of subsection 2(5) of the *Divorce Act*, the law that constitutes the comprehensive guidelines for the Province of Manitoba is the *Child Support Guidelines Regulation*, Man. Reg. 58/98.

## Coming into Force

### 3

This Order comes into force on June 1, 1998.
