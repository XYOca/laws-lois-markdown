> Reservation to the Crown Waiver Order (Great Slave Lake, N.W.T.)


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to paragraph 13(b) of the *Territorial Lands Act*[^], hereby orders that the lands described in the annexed schedule lying within 30.48 metres (100 feet in width) of the ordinary high water mark along the shoreline of Great Slave Lake, in the Northwest Territories, be included in any grant of those lands.
