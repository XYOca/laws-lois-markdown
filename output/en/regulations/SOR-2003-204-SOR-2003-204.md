> Continuation of Amalgamated or Merged Corporations Regulations


## Prescribed Purposes

### 1

For the purposes of section 213 of the *Excise Act, 2001*, the purpose of applying any provision of Parts 5 and 6, other than section 213, of that Act is a prescribed purpose.

## Coming into Force

### 2

These Regulations take effect on July 1, 2003.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to paragraph 304(1)(o) of the *Excise Act, 2001*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Continuation of Amalgamated or Merged Corporations Regulations*.
