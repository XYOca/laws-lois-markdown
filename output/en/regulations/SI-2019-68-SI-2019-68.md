> Order Designating the Prime Minister as the Minister responsible for that Act


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 55(a) of the *National Security and Intelligence Review Agency Act*[^], designates the Prime Minister as the Minister responsible for that Act.
