> Special Duty Area Pension Order


## Designation

### 1

For the purpose of the *Pension Act*, an area set out in column 1 of the schedule is designated as a special duty area in respect of the period commencing on the date set out in column 2 and terminating on the date set out in column 3.

## Repeal

### 2

[Repeal]

## Coming into Force

### 3

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Defence and the Treasury Board, pursuant to subsection 91.1(1)[^] of the *Pension Act*, hereby makes the annexed *Special Duty Area Pension Order*.
