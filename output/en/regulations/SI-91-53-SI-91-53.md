> Order Giving Notice that a Tax Agreement Between Canada and Papua New Guinea Came into Force on December 21, 1989


> Notice is hereby given, pursuant to section 18 of the *Canada-Papua New Guinea Income Tax Agreement Act, 1989*[^], that the Agreement between Canada and Papua New Guinea for the Avoidance of Double Taxation and the Prevention of Fiscal Evasion with respect to Taxes on Income came into force on December 21, 1989.
