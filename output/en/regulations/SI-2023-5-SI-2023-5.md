> Order Dissolving the Canadian Securities Regulation Regime Transition Office


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, under subsection 17(1)[^] of the *Canadian Securities Regulation Regime Transition Office Act*[^], dissolves the Canadian Securities Regulation Regime Transition Office effective March 30, 2023.
