> Regulations Defining “EU country or other CETA beneficiary”


## Interpretation

### 1

For the purposes of the *Customs Tariff*, **EU country or other CETA beneficiary** means a country or territory set out in the schedule to these Regulations.

## Coming into Force

### [^]2

These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 97 of the *Canada–European Union Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement Implementation Act*, chapter 6 of the Statutes of Canada, 2017, comes into force, but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 49.9[^] of the *Customs Tariff*[^], makes the annexed *Regulations Defining **EU country or other CETA beneficiary***.
