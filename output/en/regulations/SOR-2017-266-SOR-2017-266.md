> Critical Habitat of the Northern Abalone (Haliotis kamtschatkana) Order


### 1 Application

Subsection 58(1) of the *Species at Risk Act* applies to the critical habitat of the Northern Abalone (Haliotis kamtschatkana), which is identified in the action plan for that species that is included in the Species at Risk Public Registry.

### 2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
