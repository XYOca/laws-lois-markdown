> Transfer of the Communications Security Establishment in the Department of National Defence Regulations


## Block Transfer

### 1

Subsection 132(1) of the *Public Service Employment Act* applies to all persons employed or engaged in the portion of the federal public administration in the Department of National Defence known as the Communications Security Establishment.

## Coming into Force

### 2

These Regulations come into force on November 16, 2011.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to subsection 123(1) of the *Public Service Employment Act*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Transfer of the Communications Security Establishment in the Department of National Defence Regulations*.
