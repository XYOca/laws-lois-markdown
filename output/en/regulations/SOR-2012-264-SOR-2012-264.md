> Regulations for the Redemption of One Cent Coins


### 1 Authorization

The Minister is authorized to redeem one cent coins that are or that have at any time been current in Canada.

### 2 Coming into force

These Regulations come into force on the day of their registration.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 9.01(1)[^] of the *Currency Act*[^], makes the annexed *Regulations for the Redemption of One Cent Coins*.
