# Social Insurance Number Disclosure Regulations

> Regulations Respecting the Disclosure of Social Insurance Numbers for the Purpose of Registration under Section 240 of the Excise Tax Act


## Short Title

### 1

These Regulations may be cited as the *Social Insurance Number Disclosure Regulations*.

## Disclosure Requirement

### 2

Every individual who applies to the Minister to be registered pursuant to section 240 of the *Excise Tax Act* in the form prescribed by the Minister for that purpose shall disclose therein the individual’s Social Insurance Number.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to paragraph 277(1)(d)[^] of the *Excise Tax Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations respecting the disclosure of Social Insurance Numbers for the purpose of registration under section 240 of the Excise Tax Act*.
