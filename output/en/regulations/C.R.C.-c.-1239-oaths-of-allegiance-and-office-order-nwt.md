# Oaths of Allegiance and Office Order (N.W.T.)

> Order prescribing oaths of allegiance and office


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *Oaths of Allegiance and Office Order (N.W.T.)*.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- 2014, c. 2, s. 59

</details>

## Oath of Allegiance

### 2

The oath of allegiance prescribed by the *Oaths of Allegiance Act* is the oath of allegiance to be taken and subscribed by the Commissioner of the Northwest Territories.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- 2014, c. 2, s. 60

</details>

## Oath of Office

### 3

The oath of office to be taken and subscribed by the Commissioner of the Northwest Territories is in the form set out in Schedule I.

### 4

[Repealed, 2014, c. 2, s. 61]

## Swearing

### 5

The oath of allegiance and oath of office of the Commissioner of the Northwest Territories shall be sworn and subscribed before the Clerk or Assistant Clerk of the Privy Council.
