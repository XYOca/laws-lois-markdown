> Oil Product Designation Regulations


### 1

[Repealed, SOR/2020-52, s. 1]

## Designation

### 2

The following substances are designated as oil products:

- (a) methanol; and

- (b) methyl tertiary butyl ether.

## Exemption

### 3

Methanol and methyl tertiary butyl ether are exempt from the operation of Part 9 of the *Canadian Energy Regulator Act*.
