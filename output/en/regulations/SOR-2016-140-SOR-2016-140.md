> Ferry-Boats Remission Order, 2016


## Remission

### 1 Ferry-boats

Remission is granted of the customs duties paid or payable under the *Customs Tariff* in respect of ferry-boats, classified under subheading No. 8901.10 in the List of Tariff Provisions set out in the schedule to the *Customs Tariff*, excluding those produced in Canada that have been exported and then subsequently re-imported into Canada.

### 2 Conditions

The remission is granted on the following conditions:

- (a) the ferry-boat was permanently imported into Canada for the first time on or after October 1, 2015;

- (b) a claim for remission is made by the importer to the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness within two years after the date of importation; and

- (c) the importer files, on request, all evidence that is required by the Canada Border Services Agency to determine eligibility for remission.
