> Equivalent Courses (GST/HST) Regulations


### 1

[Repealed, SOR/2002-277, s. 5]

## Prescribed Courses

### 2

For the purpose of section 9 of Part III of Schedule V to the *Excise Tax Act*, music lessons are prescribed equivalents of music courses that follow a curriculum designated by a school authority.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/94-368, s. 4

</details>

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 277(1)[^] of the *Excise Tax Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations prescribing equivalents of courses that follow a curriculum designated by a school authority*.
