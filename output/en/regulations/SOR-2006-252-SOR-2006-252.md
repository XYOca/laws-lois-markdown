> Period for Entering into an Agreement for the Purpose of Jointly Establishing a Review Panel Regulations


## Interpretation

### 1

In these Regulations, **Act** means the *Mackenzie Valley Resource Management Act*.

## Time Limits

### 2

For the purpose of subsection 138.1(4) of the Act, the period is 90 days after the day on which the proposal is referred to the Minister of the Environment under paragraph 130(1)(c) of the Act.

### 3

For the purpose of subsection 141(4) of the Act, the period is 90 days after the day on which an environmental impact review of the proposal is ordered under subparagraph 128(1)(b)(i), paragraph 128(1)(c) or 130(1)(a), subparagraph 130(1)(b)(ii) or paragraph 131(1)(b) of the Act.

## Coming into Force

### 4

These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
