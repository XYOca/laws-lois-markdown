# Pacific Hake Exemption Notice

> Notice Excepting Certain Pacific Hake from the Operation of Section 29 of the Fisheries Act


## Short Title

### 1

This Notice may be cited as the *Pacific Hake Exemption Notice*.

## Exemption

### 2

Pacific hake caught in those waters described in the *Pacific Fishery Management Area Regulations* as Areas 121, 123, 124, 125 and 126 are hereby excepted from the operation of section 29 of the *Fisheries Act*.

> The Minister of Fisheries and Oceans, pursuant to section 29 of the *Fisheries Act*, hereby makes the annexed *Notice excepting certain Pacific hake from operation of section 29 of the *Fisheries Act**.
