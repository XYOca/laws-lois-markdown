> Order Transferring from the Department of Fisheries and Oceans to the Department of Transport of Certain Portions of the Directorate-General of Maritime Programs


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, hereby transfers from the Department of Fisheries and Oceans to the Department of Transport the control and supervision of those portions of the Directorate-General of Marine Programs that are responsible for policy related to pleasure craft safety, marine navigation services, pollution prevention and response, and navigable waters, effective December 12, 2003.
