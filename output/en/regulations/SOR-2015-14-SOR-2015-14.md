> Order Fixing the Day for the Purposes of Sections 131, 132, 134, 136 and 137 of the Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1


## General

### 1 Day for purposes of certain sections

The day for the purposes of sections 131, 132, 134, 136 and 137 of the *Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1*[^] is fixed as September 1, 2018.

- SOR/2017-93, s. 1

## Coming into Force

### [^]2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the first day on which both sections 114 and 115 of the *Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1*[^] are in force, but if it is registered after that day, it comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to sections 131, 132, 134, 136 and 137 of the *Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1*[^], makes the annexed *Order Fixing the Day for the Purposes of Sections 131, 132, 134, 136 and 137 of the Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1*.
