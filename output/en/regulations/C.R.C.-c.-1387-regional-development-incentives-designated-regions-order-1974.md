# Regional Development Incentives Designated Regions Order, 1974

> Order Designating Regions for the Purposes of the Regional Development Incentives Act


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *Regional Development Incentives Designated Regions Order, 1974*.

## Designation

### 2

The regions set out in the schedule are hereby designated as designated regions for the purposes of the *Regional Development Incentives Act* until December 31, 1984.
