> Secretary of State Authority to Prescribe Fees Order (Student Loans)


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Secretary of State of Canada and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19.1(b)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*, is pleased hereby to authorize the Secretary of State of Canada to prescribe, by order, a fee based on a percentage of the amount of the loan, to be paid by full-time students to whom guaranteed student loans are made after July 31, 1991 under the *Canada Student Loans Act*.
