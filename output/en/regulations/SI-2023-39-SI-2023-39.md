> Order Transferring from the Minister of Infrastructure and Communities to the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness the Control and Supervision of the Canadian Intergovernmental Conference Secretariat


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, under paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the Minister of Infrastructure and Communities to the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness the control and supervision of the portion of the federal public administration known as the Canadian Intergovernmental Conference Secretariat.
