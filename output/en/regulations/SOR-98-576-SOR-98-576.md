> Capacity Plates and Conformity Plates Charges Order


## Interpretation

### 1

The definitions in this section apply in this Order.

**capacity plate** means a plate pertaining to the recommended safe limits of engine power and recommended gross load capacity. (**plaque de capacité**)

**conformity plate** means a notice in the form of a plate that pertains to compliance with construction standards. (**plaque de conformité**)

## Charges

### 2

The charge to be paid for the issue of a capacity plate is $5.

### 3

The charge to be paid for the issue of a conformity plate is $5.

## Coming into Force

### 4

This Order comes into force on April 1, 1999.

> The Minister of Transport, pursuant to Order in Council P.C. 1994-642 of April 21, 1994[^], made pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(b)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*, hereby makes the annexed *Capacity Plates and Conformity Plates Charges Order*.
