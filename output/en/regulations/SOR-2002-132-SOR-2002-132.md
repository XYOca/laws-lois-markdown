> Entity Member of Group Regulations


## Entity Member of a Bank’s Group

### 1 Entity member of bank’s group *(Entité membre du groupe d’une banque)*

For the purpose of paragraph 464(2)(d) of the *Bank Act*, the following entities are members of a bank’s group:

- (a) a central for which an order has been made under subsection 473(1) of the *Cooperative Credit Associations Act* that controls the bank;

- (b) a subsidiary of a central referred to in paragraph (a); and

- (c) an entity in which a central referred to in paragraph (a) has a substantial investment.

## Entity Member of a Bank Holding Company’s Group

### 2 Entity member of bank holding company’s group *(Entité membre du groupe d’une société de portefeuille bancaire)*

- (b) a subsidiary of a central referred to in paragraph (a); and

- (c) an entity in which a central referred to in paragraph (a) has a substantial investment.
