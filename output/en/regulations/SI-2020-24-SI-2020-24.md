> Order Designating the Minister of Labour as the Minister for the Purposes of that Act


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 5 of the *Pay Equity Act*[^], designates the Minister of Labour, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, as the Minister for the purposes of that Act.
