# Canadian Jewish Heritage Month Act

> An Act respecting Canadian Jewish Heritage Month


## Short title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Canadian Jewish Heritage Month Act*.

## Canadian Jewish Heritage Month

### 2 Canadian Jewish Heritage Month

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the month of May is to be known as “Canadian Jewish Heritage Month”.
