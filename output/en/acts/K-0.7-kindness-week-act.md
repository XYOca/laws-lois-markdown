# Kindness Week Act

> An Act respecting Kindness Week


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Kindness Week Act*.

## Kindness Week

### 2 Kindness Week

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the third week of February is to be known as “Kindness Week”.
