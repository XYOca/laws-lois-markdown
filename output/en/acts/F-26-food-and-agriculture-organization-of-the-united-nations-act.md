# Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations Act

> An Act for carrying into effect the Agreement for a Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations between Canada and certain other Nations and Authorities


### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations Act*.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- R.S., c. F-26, s. 1

</details>

### 2 Constitution approved

The Constitution of the Food and Agriculture Organization set out in the schedule is hereby approved.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- R.S., c. F-26, s. 2

</details>

### 3 Powers of Governor in Council

The Governor in Council may make such appointments, establish such offices, make such orders in council and do such things as appear to him to be necessary for carrying out the provisions of the Constitution set out in the schedule.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- R.S., c. F-26, s. 2

</details>

### 4 Annual report

The Minister of Foreign Affairs shall prepare a report of the operations under this Act, and cause it to be laid before Parliament, as soon as practicable after the close of each fiscal year, but in any event within ninety days after the end of each fiscal year, or, if Parliament is not sitting on the ninetieth day and the report has not been laid before Parliament, the Minister of Foreign Affairs shall cause it to be laid before Parliament on any of the first fifteen days thereafter that either House of Parliament is sitting.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- R.S., 1985, c. F-26, s. 4; 1995, c. 5, s. 25

</details>
