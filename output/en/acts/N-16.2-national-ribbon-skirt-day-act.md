# National Ribbon Skirt Day Act

> An Act respecting a National Ribbon Skirt Day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *National Ribbon Skirt Day Act*.

## National Ribbon Skirt Day

### 2 National Ribbon Skirt Day

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the 4th day of January shall be known as “National Ribbon Skirt Day”.
