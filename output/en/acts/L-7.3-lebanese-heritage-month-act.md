# Lebanese Heritage Month Act

> An Act respecting Lebanese Heritage Month


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Lebanese Heritage Month Act*.

## Lebanese Heritage Month

### 2 Lebanese Heritage Month

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the month of November is to be known as “Lebanese Heritage Month”.
