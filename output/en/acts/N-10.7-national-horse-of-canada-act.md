# National Horse of Canada Act

> An Act to provide for the recognition of the Canadian horse as the national horse of Canada


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *National Horse of Canada Act*.

## The National Horse

### 2 National horse

The horse known as the Canadian horse is hereby recognized and declared to be the national horse of Canada.
