# Holidays Act

> An Act respecting Holidays


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Holidays Act*.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- R.S., c. H-7, s. 1

</details>

## Canada Day

### 2 Canada Day

(1) July 1, not being a Sunday, is a legal holiday and shall be kept and observed as such throughout Canada under the name of "*Canada Day*".

**When July 1 is a Sunday**

(2) When July 1 is a Sunday, July 2 is a legal holiday and shall be kept and observed as such throughout Canada under the name of "*Canada Day*".

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- R.S., c. H-7, s. 2; 1980-81-82-83, c. 124, s. 1

</details>

## Remembrance Day

### 3 Remembrance Day

November 11, being the day in the year 1918 on which the Great War was triumphantly concluded by an armistice, is a legal holiday and shall be kept and observed as such throughout Canada under the name of “*Remembrance Day*”.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- R.S., 1985, c. H-5, s. 3; 2018, c. 3, s. 1

</details>

## Victoria Day

### 4 Victoria Day

The first Monday immediately preceding May 25 is a legal holiday and shall be kept and observed as such throughout Canada under the name of "*Victoria Day*".

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- R.S., c. H-7, s. 4

</details>
