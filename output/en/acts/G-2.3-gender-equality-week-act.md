# Gender Equality Week Act

> An Act to establish Gender Equality Week


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Gender Equality Week Act*.

## Gender Equality Week

### 2 Gender Equality Week

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the fourth week in September is to be known as “Gender Equality Week”.
