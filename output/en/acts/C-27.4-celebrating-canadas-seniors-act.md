# Celebrating Canada’s Seniors Act

> An Act to establish National Seniors Day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Celebrating Canada’s Seniors Act*.

## National Seniors Day

### 2 Designation

Throughout Canada, in each year, October 1 is to be known as National Seniors Day.

### 3 Not holiday or non-juridical day

For greater certainty, National Seniors Day is not a holiday or a non-juridical day.
