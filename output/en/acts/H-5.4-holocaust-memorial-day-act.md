# Holocaust Memorial Day Act

> An Act to establish Holocaust Memorial Day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Holocaust Memorial Day Act*.

## Holocaust Memorial Day

### 2 Holocaust Memorial Day — Yom ha-Shoah

Yom ha-Shoah or the Day of the Holocaust, as determined in each year by the Jewish lunar calendar, is proclaimed as “Holocaust Memorial Day — Yom ha-Shoah”.
