# New Brunswick Indian Reserves Agreement Act

> An Act to confirm an Agreement between the Government of Canada and the Government of the Province of New Brunswick respecting Indian Reserves


### 1 Agreement ratified and confirmed

The Agreement between the Government of Canada and the Government of the Province of New Brunswick, set out in the Schedule, is ratified and confirmed, and it shall take effect according to its terms.
