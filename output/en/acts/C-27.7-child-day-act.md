# Child Day Act

> An Act respecting a national child day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Child Day Act*.

## Child Day

### 2 Child day

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the 20th day of November, being the day in the year 1989 when the United Nations Convention on the Rights of the Child was adopted in the United Nations General Assembly, shall be known as "Child Day".
