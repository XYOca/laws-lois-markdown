> Order Transferring to the Department of External Affairs from the Department of the Solicitor General, the Control and Supervision of the Passport Office


> Whereas, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, the control and supervision of the Passport Office was transferred from the Department of External Affairs to the Department of the Solicitor General by Order in Council P.C. 1993-1485 of June 25, 1993[^];
