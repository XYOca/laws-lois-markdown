> Order Transferring from the Minister of Finance to the Minister of Industry, the Powers, Duties and Functions in Relation to Regional Economic Development in Quebec and the Control and Supervision of the Economic Development Agency of Canada for the Regions of Quebec


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, is pleased hereby to transfer from the Minister of Finance to the Minister of Industry
