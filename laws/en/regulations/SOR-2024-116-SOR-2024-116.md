> Order Grouping Ministers’ Offices for the Purpose of a Pay Equity Plan


### 1 Grouping of ministers’ offices

All ministers’ offices are grouped for the purpose of establishing and updating a single pay equity plan.

### 2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Labour, makes the annexed *Order Grouping Ministers’ Offices for the Purpose of a Pay Equity Plan* under subsection 173(1) of the *Pay Equity Act*[^].
