> Order Designating the Minister of Industry, who is a Federal Minister, to be the Minister for the Purposes of that Act


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 4 of the *College of Patent Agents and Trade-mark Agents Act*[^], designates the Minister of Industry, who is a federal minister, to be the Minister for the purposes of that Act.
