> Adjustment of Fees Paid or Payable Under the Customs Brokers Licensing Regulations for the 2019–2020 to 2025–2026 Fiscal Years Remission Order


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, considering that the collection of the amounts is unreasonable, on the recommendation of the Treasury Board and the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness, under subsection 23(2.1)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*[^], remits the amounts of the annual adjustments — made under subsection 17(1) of the *Service Fees Act*[^] — to the fees paid or payable under subsections 11(1)[^] and (2)[^] and paragraph 16(b)[^] of the *Customs Brokers Licensing Regulations*[^] for the 2019–2020 to 2025–2026 fiscal years.
