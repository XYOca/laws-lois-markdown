> Critical Habitat of the Round Hickorynut (Obovaria subrotunda) Order


## Application

### 1

Subsection 58(1) of the *Species at Risk Act* applies to the critical habitat of the Round Hickorynut (Obovaria subrotunda) which is identified in the recovery strategy for that species that is included in the Species at Risk Public Registry.

## Coming into Force

### 2

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
