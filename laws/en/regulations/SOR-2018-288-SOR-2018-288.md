> In-Transit Steel Goods Remission Order


### 1 Remission

Subject to subsection (2), remission is granted of the surtax paid or payable under the *Order Imposing a Surtax on the Importation of Certain Steel Goods*[^] in respect of goods that were in transit to Canada before October 25, 2018.

### 2 Conditions

The remission is granted if

- (a) the good is imported into Canada on or after October 25, 2018; and

- (b) a claim for remission is made to the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness within two years after the date of importation.

### 3 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 115[^] of the *Customs Tariff*[^], makes the annexed *In-Transit Steel Goods Remission Order*.
