> MicroSD Cards Exclusion Regulations (Copyright Act)


## Microsd Cards

### 1

Memory cards in microSD form factor, including microSD, microSDHC and microSDXC cards, are excluded from the definition **audio recording medium** in section 79 of the *Copyright Act*.

## Coming into Force

### 2

These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Industry, pursuant to sections 79[^] and 87[^] of the *Copyright Act*[^], makes the annexed *MicroSD Cards Exclusion Regulations (Copyright Act)*.
