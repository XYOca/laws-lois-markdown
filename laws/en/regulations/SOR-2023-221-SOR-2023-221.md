> Critical Habitat of the Atlantic Mud-piddock (Barnea truncata) Order


### 1 Application

Subsection 58(1) of the *Species at Risk Act* applies to the critical habitat of the Atlantic Mud-piddock (Barnea truncata), which is identified in the recovery strategy for that species that is included in the Species at Risk Public Registry.

### 2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
