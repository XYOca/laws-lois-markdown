> Order Transferring from the Minister of State (Tourism) to the Minister of Industry the Powers, Duties and Functions Under the Canadian Tourism Commission Act


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, under paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers to the Minister of Industry the powers, duties and functions under the *Canadian Tourism Commission Act*[^] that were transferred to the Minister of State (Tourism) by Order in Council P.C. 2023-769 of July 26, 2023[^].
