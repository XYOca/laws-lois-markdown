> Diversion of Imported Goods Exemption Regulations


## Interpretation

### 1

The definitions in this section apply in these Regulations.

**Act** means the *Customs Act*. (**Loi**)

**emergency** means a medical emergency, fire, flood or any disaster that threatens life, property or the environment. (**urgence**)

## Exemption for Emergency Use

### 2

Imported goods that, in response to an emergency, are temporarily diverted to a use other than that for which they were released are exempt from the operation of subsection 32.2(6) of the Act for the period during which the goods are used in response to the emergency.

### 3

Imported goods that are consumed in response to an emergency are exempt from the operation of subsection 32.2(6) of the Act.
