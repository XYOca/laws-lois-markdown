> Order Designating the Minister of Justice as the Minister for the Purposes of that Act


> His Excellency the Administrator of the Government of Canada in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 3 of the *United Nations Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples Act*[^], designates the Minister of Justice, who is a federal minister, to be the Minister for the purposes of that Act.
