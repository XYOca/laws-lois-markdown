> Minister of National Revenue Authority to Prescribe Fees Order


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(b)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*, is pleased hereby to authorize the Minister of National Revenue to prescribe, by order, the fees or charges to be paid by persons who request that publications of the Department of National Revenue be distributed to them.
