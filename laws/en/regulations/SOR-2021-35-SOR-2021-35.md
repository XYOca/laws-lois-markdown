> Canada Recovery Benefits Regulations


### 1 Definition of **Act**

In these Regulations, **Act** means the *Canada Recovery Benefits Act*.

### 2 Canada Recovery Benefit

For the purpose of subsection 9(1) of the Act, the maximum number of two-week periods in respect of which a Canada recovery benefit is payable to a person is 27.

### 4 Prescribed income

For the purposes of subparagraphs 10(1)(g)(iv) and 17(1)(g)(iv) of the Act, the benefit set out in section 9.1 of the Act is prescribed income.

### 5 Coming into force

These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> His Excellency the Administrator of the Government of Canada in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Employment and Social Development, pursuant to subsections 9(2), 16(2) and 23(4) of the *Canada Recovery Benefits Act*[^], makes the annexed *Canada Recovery Benefits Regulations*.
