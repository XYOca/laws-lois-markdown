> Student Employment Programs Participants Exclusion Approval Order


> Whereas the Public Service Commission has decided that it is neither practicable nor in the best interests of the public service to apply certain provisions of the *Public Service Employment Act*[^] to participants in the Federal Student Work Experience Program, the Research Affiliate Program, the Post-Secondary Co-op/Internship Program or any other student employment program established by the Treasury Board after consultation with the Public Service Commission, for the purposes of hiring and during the course of their employment under the program;
