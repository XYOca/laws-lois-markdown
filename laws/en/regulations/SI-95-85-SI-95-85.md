> Order Transferring to the Department of Agriculture and Agri-Food the Control and Supervision of the Portion of the Public Service known as the Retail Food Program of the Department of Industry


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, is pleased hereby to transfer to the Department of Agriculture and Agri-Food the control and supervision of the portion of the public service in the Department of Industry known as the Retail Food Program.
