# National Energy Board Order No. MO-62-69

> Order Respecting the Application of Part IV of the National Energy Board Act


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *National Energy Board Order No. MO-62-69*.

## Application of part IV of the act

### 2

The National Energy Board, pursuant to subsection 97(1)[^] of the *National Energy Board Act*, hereby orders that Part IV of the said Act applies to every person who was operating a pipeline on November 2, 1959.
