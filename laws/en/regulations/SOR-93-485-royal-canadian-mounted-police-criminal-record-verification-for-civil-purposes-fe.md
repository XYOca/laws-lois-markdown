# Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Criminal Record Verification for Civil Purposes Fee Regulations

> Regulations Prescribing the Fee To Be Paid for the Verification of a Criminal Record for Civil Purposes


## Short Title

### 1

These Regulations may be cited as the *Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Criminal Record Verification for Civil Purposes Fee Regulations*.

## Fee

### 2

The fee to be paid by an individual for the verification by the Royal Canadian Mounted Police of a criminal record for civil purposes is $25.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Solicitor General of Canada and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(a)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations prescribing the fee to be paid for the verification of a criminal record for civil purposes*.
