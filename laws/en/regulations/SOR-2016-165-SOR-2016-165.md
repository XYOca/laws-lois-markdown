> Candles Regulations


## Requirement

### 1 Spontaneous reignition

A candle must not reignite spontaneously after it is extinguished.

## Coming into Force

### 3 Registration

These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the *Canada Consumer Product Safety Act*[^], makes the annexed *Candles Regulations*.
