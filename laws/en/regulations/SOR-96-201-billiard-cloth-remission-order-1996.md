# Billiard Cloth Remission Order, 1996

> Order Respecting the Remission of Customs Duties on Billiard Cloth for the Manufacture or Repair of Billiard Tables


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *Billiard Cloth Remission Order, 1996*.

## Remission

### 2

Subject to section 3, remission is hereby granted of the customs duties paid or payable under Schedule I to the *Customs Tariff* on billiard cloth, of tariff item Nos. 5111.19.00 or 5112.19.10, for the manufacture or repair of billiard tables.

## Conditions

### 3

The remission is granted pursuant to section 2 on the condition that:

- (a) the billiard cloth is imported into Canada during the period commencing on July 1, 1995 and ending on December 31, 1997; and

- (b) a claim for remission is made to the Minister of National Revenue within three years of the date of importation of the goods for which remission is claimed.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 101 of the *Customs Tariff*[^], is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Order respecting the remission of customs duties on billiard cloth for the manufacture or repair of billiard tables*.
