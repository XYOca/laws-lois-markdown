# Government Employees Land Acquisition Order, 1996, No. 2

> Order Authorizing a Certain Employee of the Government of Canada to Acquire an Interest in Territorial Lands in the Northwest Territories


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *Government Employees Land Acquisition Order, 1996, No. 2*.

## Authorization

### 2

The employee of the Government of Canada named in the schedule is hereby authorized to acquire an interest in territorial lands in the Northwest Territories as described in the schedule.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to paragraph 29(1)(a) of the *Territorial Lands Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Order authorizing a certain employee of the Government of Canada to acquire an interest in territorial lands in the Northwest Territories*.
