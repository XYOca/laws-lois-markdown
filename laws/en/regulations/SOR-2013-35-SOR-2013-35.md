> Exemption Order for Certain Licences, Authorizations and Documents (Westslope Cutthroat Trout (Alberta Population))


## Exemption

### 1

Section 32 of the *Species at Risk Act* does not apply to the following licences, authorizations and documents for a period of one year from the date that the Westslope Cutthroat Trout (Alberta population) is added to the List of Wildlife Species at Risk set out in Schedule 1 to that Act:

- (a) the *Alberta Fishery Regulations, 1998* to the extent that they authorize sportfishing;

- (b) the licences issued under subsection 13(3) of the *Alberta Fishery Regulations, 1998*; and

- (c) the *National Parks of Canada Fishing Regulations* to the extent that they authorize fishing.

## Coming into Force

### 2

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Fisheries and Oceans, pursuant to section 76 of the *Species at Risk Act*[^], makes the annexed *Exemption Order for Certain Licences, Authorizations and Documents (Westslope Cutthroat Trout (Alberta Population))*.
