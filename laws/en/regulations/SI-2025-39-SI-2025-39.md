> Order Transferring from the Minister of Foreign Affairs to the Minister for International Trade the Powers, Duties and Functions Under Subsection 53(2) of the Customs Tariff


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, under paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the Minister of Foreign Affairs to the Minister for International Trade the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Foreign Affairs under subsection 53(2)[^] of the *Customs Tariff*[^].
