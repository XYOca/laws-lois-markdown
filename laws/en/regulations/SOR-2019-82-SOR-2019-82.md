> Order Extending the Application of Section 14 of that Act for a Period of Five Years


> The Minister of Citizenship and Immigration, pursuant to subsection 28.1(1)[^] of the *Citizenship Act*[^], extends the application of section 14 of that Act for a period of five years, ending on July 31, 2024.
