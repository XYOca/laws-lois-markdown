> Order Designating the Deputy Minister of the Department of Indigenous Services Canada as Deputy Head in respect of that Entity


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(a)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*[^], designates the Deputy Minister of the Department of Indigenous Services Canada as deputy head in respect of that entity, effective November 30, 2017.
