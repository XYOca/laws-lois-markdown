> Income Support Payment (Number of Weeks) Regulations


## Maximum Number of Weeks

### 1 28 weeks

For the purpose of subsection 8(1) of the *Canada Emergency Response Benefit Act*, the maximum number of weeks for which income support payments may be made to a worker is 28 weeks.

### 2 Registration
