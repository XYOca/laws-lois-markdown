# Grosse Isle, P.Q., Prohibited Place Order

> Order Declaring that Grosse Isle, P.Q., Is a Prohibited Place


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as *Grosse Isle, P.Q., Prohibited Place Order*.

## Declaration

### 2

Grosse Isle, County of Montmagny, Province of Quebec is hereby declared to be a prohibited place within the meaning of the *Foreign Interference and Security of Information Act*.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- 2001, c. 41, s. 39
- 2024, c. 16, s. 57

</details>

## Application

### 3

This Order does not apply to over-flying aircraft.
