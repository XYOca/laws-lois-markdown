> Qalipu Mi’kmaq First Nation Band Order


## Declaration

### 1

The body of Indians described in section 2 is declared to be a band for the purposes of the *Indian Act*, to be known as the Qalipu Mi’kmaq First Nation.

### 2

The Qalipu Mi’kmaq First Nation is the body of Indians consisting of the persons identified in the Schedule.
