> Transfer of Duties Order


> His Excellency the Administrator of the Government of Canada in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the Minister of National Revenue to the Minister of the Environment the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of National Revenue under section 188 of the *Greenhouse Gas Pollution Pricing Act*[^].
