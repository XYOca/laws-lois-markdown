> Weed Seeds Order, 2016


### 1

The seeds of the kinds of plants set out in the schedule are specified to be weed seeds for the purposes of the *Seeds Act*.

## Repeal

### 2

[Repeal]

## Coming into Force

### 3

These Regulations come into force on November 1, 2016.

> The Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food, pursuant to subsection 4(2)[^] of the *Seeds Act*[^], makes the annexed *Weed Seeds Order, 2016*.
