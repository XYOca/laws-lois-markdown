# Submission of Samples of Fabrics for Importation Regulations

> Regulations Respecting the Submission of Samples of Fabrics for Importation


## Short Title

### 1

These Regulations may be cited as the *Submission of Samples of Fabrics for Importation Regulations*.

## Application

### 2

These Regulations do not apply in respect of the imported fabrics listed in the schedule.

## Sample

### 3

Every person importing fabrics into Canada shall ensure that there is attached to the accounting document required pursuant to subsection 32(1) of the *Customs Act* a sample of every quality or style of the imported fabrics.

### 4

A sample of fabric referred to in section 3 must be of a size sufficient to identify the design or pattern, if any, woven into or imprinted on the fabric and must be at least 10 × 10 cm.

> Whereas, pursuant to subsection 164(3) of the *Customs Act*, a copy of proposed Regulations, substantially in the form annexed hereto, was published in Part I of the *Canada Gazette* on March 22, 1986 and a reasonable opportunity was thereby afforded to interested persons to make representations with respect thereto to the Minister of National Revenue.
