> Transfer of Duties Order


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the Minister of Industry to the Minister of State styled the Minister of Science the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Industry under Part I of the *Budget Implementation Act, 1997*[^].
