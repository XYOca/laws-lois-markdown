> Order Giving Notice that a Tax Agreement Between Canada and the Polish People’s Republic Came into Force on November 30, 1989


> Notice is hereby given, pursuant to section 12 of the *Canada-Poland Income Tax Convention Act, 1989*[^], that the Convention between the Government of Canada and the Government of the Polish People’s Republic for the Avoidance of Double Taxation with respect to Taxes on Income and on Capital came into force on November 30, 1989.
