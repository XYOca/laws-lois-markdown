## Coming into Force

### 2

These Regulations come into force on June 18, 1998.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to subsection 26(4) of the *Canada Petroleum Resources Act*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Lancaster Sound Designated Area Regulations*.
