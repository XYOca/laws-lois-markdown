> Reservation to the Crown Waiver Order (Bed of Great Slave Lake)


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to section 14 of the *Territorial Lands Act*, hereby orders that the bed, below the ordinary high water mark, of a body of water as described in the annexed schedule, located at Great Slave Lake, in the Northwest Territories, be included in the grant of certain territorial lands.
