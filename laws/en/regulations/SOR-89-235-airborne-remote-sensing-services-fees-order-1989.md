# Airborne Remote Sensing Services Fees Order, 1989

> Order Prescribing the Fees and Charges to be Paid for the Provision of Airborne Remote Sensing Services


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *Airborne Remote Sensing Services Fees Order, 1989*.

## Interpretation

### 2

In this Order,

**CCT** means computer-compatible tape; (**BM**)

**HDDT** means high density digital tape; (**BNHD**)

**NAPL** means the National Air Photo Library; (**PNA**)

**SAR** means Synthetic Aperture Radar. (**ROS**)

## Fees and Charges

### 3

Where the Minister of Energy, Mines and Resources provides a person with an airborne remote sensing service described in Column I of an item of the schedule, that person shall pay to the Minister the fees and charges set out in Column II of that item.

> The Minister of Energy, Mines and Resources, pursuant to Order in Council P.C. 1982-1125 of April 8, 1982[^] made pursuant to paragraph 13(b) of the *Financial Administration Act*, hereby revokes the *Airborne Remote Sensing Services Fees Order, 1986* made on September 2, 1986[^] and makes the annexed *Order prescribing the fees and charges to be paid for the provision of airborne remote sensing services* in substitution therefor.
