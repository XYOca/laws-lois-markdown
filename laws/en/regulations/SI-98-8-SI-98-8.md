> Charitable Goods Remission Order


## Remission

### 1

Remission is hereby granted of all the taxes paid or payable under Division III of Part IX and under any other Part of the *Excise Tax Act*, and of the customs duties paid or payable under section 21 of the *Customs Tariff*, on goods donated by a non-resident of Canada to religious, charitable or educational institutions in Canada.

## Coming into Force

### 3

This Order comes into force on January 1, 1998.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to subsection 23(2)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*, hereby makes the annexed *Charitable Goods Remission Order*.
