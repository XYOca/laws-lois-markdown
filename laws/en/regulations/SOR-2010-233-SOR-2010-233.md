> Annual Statement (Insurance Companies and Insurance Holding Companies) Regulations


## Contents of Annual Statement

### 1

The prescribed statements referred to in subsections 331(2) and 887(2) of the *Insurance Companies Act* are

- (a) a balance sheet as at the end of the financial year;

- (b) a statement of income for the financial year;

- (c) a statement of cash flows for the financial year;

- (d) a statement of changes in shareholders’ equity for the financial year; and

- (e) a statement of comprehensive income for the financial year.

## Coming into Force

### 2

These Regulations come into force on June 1, 2011.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance pursuant to subsections 331(2)[^], 887(2)[^] and 1021(1)[^] of the *Insurance Companies Act*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Annual Statement (Insurance Companies and Insurance Holding Companies) Regulations*.
