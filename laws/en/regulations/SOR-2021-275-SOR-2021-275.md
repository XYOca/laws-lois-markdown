> Lockdown Regions Designation Order (COVID-19)


### 1 Lockdown regions and benefit periods

Each region of Canada set out in column 1 of the schedule is designated as a lockdown region for the benefit period set out in column 2.

### 2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> Whereas “lockdown orders”, as defined in section 2 of the *Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit Act*[^], have been made with respect to COVID-19 outbreaks in regions set out in the annexed Order, imposing measures referred to in subparagraphs (a)(i) and (ii) of that definition that apply to those regions for at least 14 consecutive days;
