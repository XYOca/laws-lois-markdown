# Merchant Seamen Compensation Order, 1992

> Order Respecting an Increase in Compensation for Merchant Seamen and their Dependants, 1992


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *Merchant Seamen Compensation Order, 1992*.

## Interpretation

### 2

In this Order, **Act** means the *Merchant Seamen Compensation Act*. (*Loi*)

## Increase in Compensation

### 3

The amount specified in paragraph 31(1)(a) of the Act is increased to $4,800.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/95-477, s. 1; SOR/2009-104, s. 1

</details>

### 4

The amount specified in paragraph 31(1)(b) of the Act is increased to $1,000.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/95-477, s. 1

</details>

### 5

The amount specified in paragraph 31(1)(d) of the Act is increased to $1,820.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/2009-104, s. 2

</details>

### 6

The amounts specified in paragraph 31(1)(e) of the Act are increased to $1,820, $190 and $195, respectively.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/2009-104, s. 2

</details>

### 7

The amount specified in paragraph 31(1)(f) of the Act is increased to $195.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/2009-104, s. 2

</details>

### 8

The amount specified in subsection 31(3) of the Act is increased to $16,868.50.

### 9

The amount specified in paragraph 31(9)(a) of the Act is increased to $1,820.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/2009-104, s. 3

</details>

### 10

The amount of the further monthly payment for each additional child specified in paragraph 31(9)(b) of the Act is increased to $190.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/2009-104, s. 3

</details>

### 11

The amount specified in section 40 of the Act is increased to $156.32.

## Maximum Rate of Earnings

### 12

The maximum rate of earnings specified in subsection 41(1) of the Act is increased to $49,400.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- SOR/95-477, s. 2; SOR/2009-104, s. 4

</details>
