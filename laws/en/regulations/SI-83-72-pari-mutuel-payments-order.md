# Pari-Mutuel Payments Order

> Order Respecting Payments to Be Made for the Supervision of Pari-Mutuel Systems of Betting


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *Pari-Mutuel Payments Order*.

## Payments

### 2

For the purposes of subsection 188(4) of the *Criminal Code* the fraction fixed is eight-tenths of one per cent.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Agriculture and the Treasury Board, pursuant to subsection 188(4) of the *Criminal Code*, is pleased hereby to revoke the *Pari-Mutuel Payments Order* made by Order in Council P.C. 1980-3512 of 19th December, 1980[^], and to make the annexed *Order respecting payments to be made for the supervision of pari-mutuel systems of betting*, effective April 1, 1983.
