> Fee Payable for the Right to be a Citizen Remission Order


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, considering that the collection of certain amounts is unreasonable, on the recommendation of the Treasury Board and the Minister of Citizenship and Immigration, under subsection 23(2.1)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*[^], remits the excess from the adjustment, by operation of section 17[^] of the *Service Fees Act*[^], of the fee payable under section 32[^] of the *Citizenship Regulations*[^] during the period beginning on March 31, 2020 and ending on March 30, 2025.
