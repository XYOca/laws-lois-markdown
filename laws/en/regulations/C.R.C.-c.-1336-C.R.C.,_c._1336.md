> Order Designating Certain Portions of the Federal Public Administration


### 1

[Repealed, SI/2015-48, s. 2]

## Designation

### 2

The portions of the federal public administration listed in the schedule are designated as parts of the public service, and the employees in those portions of the federal public administration who are not otherwise employed in the public service are deemed, for the purposes of subsection 35(2) of the *Public Service Employment Act*, to be employed in the public service.
