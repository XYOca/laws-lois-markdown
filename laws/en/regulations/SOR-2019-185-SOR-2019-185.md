> Designated Provisions Regulations — Transportation Information (Canada Transportation Act)


## Designated Provisions

### 1

The provisions set out in the schedule are designated for the purposes of subsection 177(2) of the *Canada Transportation Act*.

## Coming into Force

### 2

These Regulations come into force on December 15, 2019.

> The Minister of Transport, pursuant to subsection 177(2)[^] of the *Canada Transportation Act*[^], makes the annexed *Designated Provisions Regulations — Transportation Information (*Canada Transportation Act*)*.
