> List of Pest Control Product Formulants and Contaminants of Health or Environmental Concern


## Interpretation

### 1

In this List, **CAS Registry Number** means the identification number that is assigned to a chemical substance by the Chemical Abstracts Service Division of the American Chemical Society.

## General

### 1.1

This List pertains only to pest control products registered under the *Pest Control Products Act*.

## Coming into Force

### [^]4

This List comes into force on the day on which section 43 of the *Pest Control Products Act*, chapter 28 of the Statutes of Canada, 2002, comes into force.

> The Minister of Health, pursuant to paragraph 43(5)(b) of the *Pest Control Products Act*[^], hereby establishes the annexed *List of Pest Control Product Formulants and Contaminants of Health or Environmental Concern*.
