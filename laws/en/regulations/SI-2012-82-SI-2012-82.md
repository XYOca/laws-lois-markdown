> Reservation to the Crown Waiver Order (Great Slave Lake, N.W.T.)


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to paragraph 13(b) of the *Territorial Lands Act*[^], orders that any portion of the lands described in the annexed schedule lying within 30.48 metres (100 feet) of the ordinary high water mark along the shoreline of Great Slave Lake, in the City of Yellowknife, in the Northwest Territories, be included in any grant of those lands.
