> Order Transferring from the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food to the Minister of National Health and Welfare, the Powers, Duties and Functions under Certain Acts


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, is pleased hereby to transfer to the Minister of National Health and Welfare the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food under the *Pest Control Products Act* and the *Pesticide Residue Compensation Act*, effective April 1, 1995.
