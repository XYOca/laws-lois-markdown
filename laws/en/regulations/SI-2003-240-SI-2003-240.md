> Order Transferring from the Minister of Transport to the Minister of National Revenue the Control and Supervision of the Royal Canadian Mint and the Powers, Duties and Functions under the Royal Canadian Mint Act


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, hereby transfers from the Minister of Transport to the Minister of National Revenue
