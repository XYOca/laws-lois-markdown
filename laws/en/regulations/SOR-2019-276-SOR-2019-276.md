> CIFTA Rules of Origin Regulations


## Rules of Origin

### 1

The following provisions of the Canada-Israel Free Trade Agreement have the force of law in Canada:

- (a) Article 3.1;

- (b) paragraph 1 of Article 3.2;

- (c) Articles 3.3 to 3.10;

- (d) paragraph 1 of Article 3.11;

- (e) Articles 3.12 to 3.14; and

- (f) Annexes 3.4 and 3.12.

## Repeal

### 2

The *CIFTA Rules of Origin Regulations*[^] are repealed.

## Coming into Force

### [^]3

These Regulations come into force on the day on which the *Act to amend the Canada-Israel Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act and to make related amendments to other Acts*, chapter 6 of the Statutes of Canada, 2019, comes into force, but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 16(2)[^] of the *Customs Tariff*[^], makes the annexed *CIFTA Rules of Origin Regulations*.
