> Order Declaring that the Reduction of Carbon Dioxide Emissions from Coal-fired Generation of Electricity Regulations Do Not Apply in Nova Scotia


## Declaration

### 1 Non-application

The *Reduction of Carbon Dioxide Emissions from Coal-fired Generation of Electricity Regulations* do not apply in Nova Scotia.

## Coming into Force

### 2 July 1, 2015

This Order comes into force on July 1, 2015.

> Whereas, pursuant to subsection 332(1)[^] of the *Canadian Environmental Protection Act, 1999*[^], the Minister of the Environment published in the *Canada Gazette*, Part I, on June 28, 2014, a copy of the proposed *Order Declaring that the Reduction of Carbon Dioxide Emissions from Coal-fired Generation of Electricity Regulations Do Not Apply in Nova Scotia*, substantially in the annexed form, and persons were given an opportunity to file comments with respect to the proposed Order or to file a notice of objection requesting that a board of review be established and stating the reasons for the objection;
