> CPAFTA Rules of Origin Regulations


## Rules of Origin

### 1

The following provisions of the Free Trade Agreement between Canada and the Republic of Panama, signed on May 14, 2010, have the force of law in Canada:

- (a) Articles 3.01 to 3.04;

- (b) Article 3.05, paragraph 1;

- (c) Articles 3.06 to 3.15; and

- (d) Annex 3.02.

## Coming into Force

### [^]2

These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 38 of the *Canada–Panama Economic Growth and Prosperity Act*, chapter 26 of the Statutes of Canada, 2012, comes into force, but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 16(2)[^] of the *Customs Tariff*[^], makes the annexed *CPAFTA Rules of Origin Regulations*.
