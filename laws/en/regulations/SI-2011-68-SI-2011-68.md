> Order Designating the President of Shared Services Canada as Deputy Head in Respect of that Entity


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(a)[^] of the *Financial Administration Act*[^], hereby designates the President of Shared Services Canada as deputy head in respect of that entity, effective August 4, 2011.
