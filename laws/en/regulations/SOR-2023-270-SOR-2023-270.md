> Order Designating Provinces Under Subsection 6(1) of the Federal Prompt Payment for Construction Work Act


### 1 Designated provinces

The following provinces are the designated provinces under subsection 6(1) of the *Federal Prompt Payment for Construction Work Act*:

- (a) Ontario;

- (b) Saskatchewan;

- (c) Alberta.

### [^]2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the day on which section 387 of the *Budget Implementation Act, 2019, No. 1*, chapter 29 of the Statutes of Canada, 2019, comes into force, but if it is registered after that day, it comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> Whereas, under subsection 6(1) of the *Federal Prompt Payment for Construction Work Act*[^], the Governor in Council is of the opinion, taking into account the criteria set out in section 2 of the *Federal Prompt Payment for Construction Work Regulations (Criteria, Time Limits, Interest and Circumstances)*, that the designated provinces have, by or under their laws, enacted.
