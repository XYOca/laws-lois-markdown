> Nova BUS Corporation Exemption Order


## Exemption

### 1

Six hundred and fifty units of Nova LFS low-floor model buses, manufactured by Nova BUS Corporation, are exempt from the requirements in subsections 217(11), (14), (17) and (19) of Schedule IV to the *Motor Vehicle Safety Regulations* that are applicable to push-out windows, for a period of two years beginning on the day on which this Order is registered under the *Statutory Instruments Act*.

## Coming into Force

### 2

This Order comes into force on September 17, 1996.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Transport, pursuant to paragraph 9(1)(c) of the *Motor Vehicle Safety Act*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Nova BUS Corporation Exemption Order*, being of the opinion that conformity with certain prescribed standards would impede the development of new kinds of buses.
