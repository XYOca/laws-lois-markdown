> Order Binding Certain Agents of Her Majesty for the Purposes of Part 1 of the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act


## Application

### 1

Part 1 of the *Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act* is binding on the following agents of Her Majesty in right of Canada to which the *Privacy Act* does not apply:

- (a) Atomic Energy of Canada Limited; and

- (b) the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation.

- (c) [Repealed, SOR/2005-250, s. 1]

## Coming into Force

### 2

This Order comes into force on January 1, 2001.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Industry, pursuant to paragraph 26(2)(a) of the *Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Order Binding Certain Agents of Her Majesty for the Purposes of Part 1 of the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act*.
