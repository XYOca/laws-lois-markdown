> Toronto Port Authority Regulations


## Prohibition

### 1

The Toronto Port Authority shall not use, or authorize or permit another person to use, the port to build a bridge or similar fixed link between the mainland of the City of Toronto and the Toronto Islands.

## Exception

### 2

Section 1 does not apply in respect of a pedestrian tunnel.
