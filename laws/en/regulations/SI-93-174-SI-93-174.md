> Order Designating the Minister of Finance as Minister for Purposes of the Act


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to the definition **Minister** in subsection 2(1) of the *Petro-Canada Public Participation Act*[^], is pleased hereby to revoke Order in Council P.C. 1991-414 of March 1, 1991[^] and to designate the Minister of Finance, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, as the Minister for the purposes of that Act.
