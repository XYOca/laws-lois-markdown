> Infant Feeding Bottle Nipples Regulations


## Interpretation

### 1 Definition of **infant feeding bottle nipple**

In these Regulations, **infant feeding bottle nipple** includes a similar product that is put in the mouth of an infant during use.

## Requirement

### 2 Limit of volatile N-nitrosamines

An infant feeding bottle nipple must not contain more than 10 µg/kg total volatile N-nitrosamines, as determined by dichloromethane extraction.
