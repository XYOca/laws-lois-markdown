> Canada Gazette Publication Order, 2014


## Form and Manner of Publishing

### 1

The *Canada Gazette* is to continue to be published electronically in Portable Document Format (PDF).

## Coming into Force

### 3

This Order comes into force on April 1, 2014.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Justice, pursuant to subsection 10(2)[^] of the *Statutory Instruments Act*[^], makes the annexed *Canada Gazette Publication Order, 2014*.
