> Order Transferring from Canada Revenue Agency to the Department of Transport the Control and Supervision of the Royal Canadian Mint and the Canada Post Corporation


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, hereby transfers from the Canada Revenue Agency to the Department of Transport the control and supervision of that portion of the federal public administration within the Canada Revenue Agency known as the Crown Corporation Secretariat, relating to the Royal Canadian Mint and the Canada Post Corporation, effective February 6, 2006.
