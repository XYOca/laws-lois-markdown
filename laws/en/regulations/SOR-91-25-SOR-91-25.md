> Artists’ Representatives (GST/HST) Regulations


### 1

[Repealed, SOR/2014-248, s. 2]

## Prescribed Registrants

### 2

Any registrant listed in the schedule is prescribed for the purposes of subsection 177(2) of the *Excise Tax Act*.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 277(1)[^] of the *Excise Tax Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations prescribing registrants who make supplies of intangible personal property on behalf of artists*.
