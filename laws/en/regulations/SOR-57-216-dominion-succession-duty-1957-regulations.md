# Dominion Succession Duty (1957) Regulations

> Regulations Respecting Section 12 of the Dominion Succession Duty Act


### 1

These Regulations may be cited as the *Dominion Succession Duty (1957) Regulations*.

### 2

The Province of Ontario and the Province of Quebec are hereby prescribed for the purposes of section 12 of the *Dominion Succession Duty Act*.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 12 of the *Dominion Succession Duty Act*, is pleased hereby to make the annexed *Regulations Respecting section 12 of the Dominion Succession Duty Act*.
