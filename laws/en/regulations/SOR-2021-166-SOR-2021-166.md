> Order Fixing the Number of Directors Under the College of Patent Agents and Trademark Agents Act


## Board of Directors

### 1 Number of directors

For the purposes of subsection 13(2) of the *College of Patent Agents and Trademark Agents Act*, the number of directors is fixed at nine.

### 2 Directors to be appointed

The number of directors that are to be appointed to the Board under subsection 13(3) of the *College of Patent Agents and Trademark Agents Act* is fixed at five.

## Coming into Force

### [^]3 S.C. 2018, c. 27, s. 247

This Order comes into force on the day on which section 13 of the *College of Patent Agents and Trademark Agents Act* comes into force, but if it is registered after that day, it comes into force on the day on which it is registered.

> The Minister of Industry, pursuant to subsections 13(2) and (3) of the *College of Patent Agents and Trademark Agents Act*[^], makes the annexed *Order Fixing the Number of Directors Under the College of Patent Agents and Trademark Agents Act*.
