> Minister of Communications Authority to Prescribe Fees Order


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Communications and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 13(b) of the *Financial Administration Act*, is pleased hereby to authorize the Minister of Communications to prescribe, by order, the fees to be paid by persons in the private sector
