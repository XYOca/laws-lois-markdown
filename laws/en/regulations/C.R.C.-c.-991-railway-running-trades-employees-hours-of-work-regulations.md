# Railway Running-Trades Employees Hours of Work Regulations

> Regulations Respecting Hours of Work of Employees Engaged in the Running-Trades in the Railway Industry in Canada


## Short Title

### 1

These Regulations may be cited as the *Railway Running-Trades Employees Hours of Work Regulations*.

## Interpretation

### 2

In these Regulations, "Act" means Part III of the *Canada Labour Code*.

## Exemption

### 3

The following classes of employees, that is to say, yardmasters, assistant yardmasters, locomotive engineers, locomotive firemen (helpers), hostlers, train conductors, train baggagemen, brakemen, yard foremen, yardmen, switch tenders and car retarder operators in those railroads that are within the legislative authority of Parliament are exempted from the application of sections 169 and 171 of the Act.

</details>

## Scheduling Hours of Work

### 4

For the purposes of these Regulations, hours of work may be scheduled and actually worked without regard to section 173 of the Act.
