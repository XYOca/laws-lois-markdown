> Regulations prescribing a territory for the purposes of the definition “country” in the Customs Tariff


## Prescription

### 1

The territory where the customs laws of Israel are applied in accordance with Article III of the Protocol on Economic Relations set out in Annex V to the Israeli-Palestinian Interim Agreement on the West Bank and the Gaza Strip, dated September 28, 1995, as that Protocol is amended from time to time, is a prescribed territory for the purposes of the definition **country** in subsection 2(1) of the *Customs Tariff*.

## Coming into Force

### [^]2

These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 41 of the *Canada-Israel Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act*, being chapter 33 of the Statutes of Canada, 1996, comes into force.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 2(3)[^] of the *Customs Tariff*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Regulations prescribing a territory for the purposes of the definition **country** in the Customs Tariff*.
