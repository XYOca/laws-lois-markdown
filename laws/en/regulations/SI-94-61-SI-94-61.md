> Order Designating the National Capital Region as the Place in Canada at which the Principal Office of the National Round Table on the Environment and the Economy Shall be Located


> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 14 of the *National Round Table on the Environment and the Economy Act*[^], is pleased hereby to designate the National Capital Region as the place in Canada at which the principal office of the National Round Table on the Environment and the Economy shall be located.
