> Order transferring from the Prime Minister to the President of the Treasury Board the control and supervision of The Leadership Network


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, hereby transfers from the Prime Minister to the President of the Treasury Board the control and supervision of that portion of the public service known as The Leadership Network.
