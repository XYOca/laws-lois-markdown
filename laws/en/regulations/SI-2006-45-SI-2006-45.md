> Order Transferring from Privy Council Office to the Department of Canadian Heritage the Control and Supervision of the Official Languages Secretariat


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*, hereby transfers from the Privy Council Office to the Department of Canadian Heritage the control and supervision of the portion of the federal public administration within the Privy Council Office in the Intergovernmental Policy Branch known as the Official Languages Secretariat, effective February 6, 2006.
