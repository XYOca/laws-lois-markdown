# Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Police Information Retrieval System Fees Order

> Order Prescribing the Fee or Charge To Be Paid by a Province or Municipality for the Use, by a Police Force for which the Province or Municipality is Responsible, of the Police Information Retrieval System Owned by the Royal Canadian Mounted Police


## Short Title

### 1

This Order may be cited as the *Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Police Information Retrieval System Fees Order*.

## Fee

### 2

A province or municipality shall pay to the Receiver General for the use, by a police force for which the province or municipality is responsible, of the Police Information Retrieval System owned by the Royal Canadian Mounted Police a fee of $450 per year in respect of each sworn police officer in the police force.

> The Solicitor General of Canada, pursuant to Order in Council P.C. 1989-1899 of September 21, 1989[^], hereby makes the annexed *Order prescribing the fee or charge to be paid by a province or municipality for the use, by a police force for which the province or municipality is responsible, of the Police Information Retrieval System owned by the Royal Canadian Mounted Police*.
