> Material Banking Group Percentage Regulations


## Prescribed Percentage

### 1 Prescribed percentage

For the purposes of subsection 508(2) of the *Bank Act*, the percentage is 35%.

### 2 Prescribed percentage

For the purposes of subsection 508(3) of the *Bank Act*, the percentage is 50%.
