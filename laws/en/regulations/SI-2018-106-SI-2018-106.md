> Order Transferring from the Department of Industry to the Privy Council Office the control and supervision of that portion of the federal public administration in the Department of Industry known as the Internal Trade Directorate


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the Department of Industry to the Privy Council Office the control and supervision of that portion of the federal public administration in the Department of Industry known as the Internal Trade Directorate.
