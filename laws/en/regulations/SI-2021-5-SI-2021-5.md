> Order Accepting the Recommendation of the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness that Each Listed Entity Remain a Listed Entity


> Whereas, pursuant to subsection 83.05(8.1)[^] of the *Criminal Code*[^], the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness has completed a review of each entity set out in the annexed schedule and listed in the *Regulations Establishing a List of Entities*[^], made pursuant to subsection 83.05(1)[^] of that Act, and has determined that there are still reasonable grounds, as set out in subsection 83.05(1)[^], for each entity to remain a listed entity;
