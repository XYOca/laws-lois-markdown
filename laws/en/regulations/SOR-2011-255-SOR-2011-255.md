> Communications Security Establishment Regulations


## Block Transfer

### 1

Subsection 132(1) of the *Public Service Employment Act* applies to the Communications Security Establishment.

## Cession of Effect

### 2

These Regulations cease to have effect at 00:00:03 on November 16, 2011.

## Coming into Force

### 3

These Regulations come into force at 00:00:01 on November 16, 2011.

> His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to subsection 123(1) of the *Public Service Employment Act*[^], hereby makes the annexed *Communications Security Establishment Regulations*.
