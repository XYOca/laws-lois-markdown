> Privacy Act Extension Order, No. 3


### 1 Extension of right of access

The right to be given access to personal information under subsection 12(1) of the *Privacy Act* is extended to include all individuals outside Canada to whom that right has not been extended previously.

### 2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on the first anniversary of the day on which it is made.

> His Excellency the Administrator of the Government of Canada in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Justice, pursuant to subsection 12(3) of the *Privacy Act*[^], makes the annexed *Privacy Act Extension Order, No. 3*.
