> Order Transferring from the Minister for International Development to the President of the King’s Privy Council for Canada the Powers, Duties and Functions in Relation to the Pacific Economic Development Agency of Canada


> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, under paragraph 2(a)[^] of the *Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act*[^], transfers from the Minister for International Development to the President of the King’s Privy Council for Canada the powers, duties and functions of the Minister for International Development in relation to the Pacific Economic Development Agency of Canada.
