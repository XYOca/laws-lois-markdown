> Order Calling in Certain Notes


### 1 Notes called in

Notes issued under the *Bank of Canada Act* in the following denominations are called in:

- (a) one dollar;

- (b) two dollars;

- (c) twenty-five dollars;

- (d) five hundred dollars; and

- (e) one thousand dollars.

### 2 Coming into force

This Order comes into force on January 1, 2021.

> Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to paragraph 9(1)(b)[^] of the *Currency Act*[^], makes the annexed *Order Calling in Certain Notes*.
