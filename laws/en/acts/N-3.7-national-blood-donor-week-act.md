# National Blood Donor Week Act

> An Act respecting a National Blood Donor Week


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *National Blood Donor Week Act*.

## National Blood Donor Week

### 2 National Blood Donor Week

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the week in which June 14 occurs shall be known as “National Blood Donor Week”.
