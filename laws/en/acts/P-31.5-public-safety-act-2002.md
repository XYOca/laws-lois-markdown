### Coming into Force

### [^]112 Coming into force

(1) Subject to subsection (2), the provisions of this Act, other than sections 1 and 109 to 111.1, and the provisions of any Act that are enacted by this Act come into force on a day or days to be fixed by order of the Governor in Council.

**Coming info force**

(2) The paragraphs or subparagraphs or any other portion of the definition **offence** in section 183 of the *Criminal Code*, as enacted by section 108 of this Act, comes into force on a day or days to be fixed by order of the Governor in Council.
