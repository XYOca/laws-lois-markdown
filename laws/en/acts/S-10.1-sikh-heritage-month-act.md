# Sikh Heritage Month Act

> An Act to designate the month of April as Sikh Heritage Month


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Sikh Heritage Month Act*.

## Sikh Heritage Month

### 2 Sikh Heritage Month

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the month of April is to be known as “Sikh Heritage Month”.
