# National Fiddling Day Act

> An Act respecting National Fiddling Day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *National Fiddling Day Act*.

## National Fiddling Day

### 2 National Fiddling Day

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the third Saturday in May is to be known as “National Fiddling Day”.

### 3 Not a legal holiday

For greater certainty, National Fiddling Day is not a legal holiday or a non-juridical day.
