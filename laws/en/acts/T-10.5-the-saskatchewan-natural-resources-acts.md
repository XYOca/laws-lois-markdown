# The Saskatchewan Natural Resources Acts

> An Act to amend The Saskatchewan Natural Resources Act


### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as *The Saskatchewan Natural Resources Act, No. 2*, and *The Saskatchewan Natural Resources Act*, chapter forty-one of the statutes of 1930 (first session), and this Act may be cited together as *The Saskatchewan Natural Resources Acts*.

### 2 Agreement confirmed

The agreement set out in the schedule hereto is hereby confirmed and shall take effect according to its terms.
