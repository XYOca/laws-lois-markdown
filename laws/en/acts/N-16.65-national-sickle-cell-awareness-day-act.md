# National Sickle Cell Awareness Day Act

> An Act respecting National Sickle Cell Awareness Day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *National Sickle Cell Awareness Day Act*.

## National Sickle Cell Awareness Day

### 2 National Sickle Cell Awareness Day

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the nineteenth day of June is to be known as “National Sickle Cell Awareness Day”.

### 3 Not a legal holiday

For greater certainty, National Sickle Cell Awareness Day is not a legal holiday or a non-juridical day.
