# Canadian Environment Week Act

> An Act respecting Canadian Environment Week


### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Canadian Environment Week Act*.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- 1970-71-72, c. 28, s. 1

</details>

### 2 Canadian Environment Week

Throughout Canada, in every year, the week that includes June 5 shall be kept and observed under the name of Canadian Environment Week.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- R.S., 1985, c. E-11, s. 2; R.S., 1985, c. 10 (1st Supp.), s. 2

</details>
