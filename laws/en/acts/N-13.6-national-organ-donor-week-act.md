# National Organ Donor Week Act

> An Act respecting a National Organ Donor Week in Canada


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *National Organ Donor Week Act*.

## National Organ Donor Week

### 2 National Organ Donor Week

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the last full week of April shall be known under the name of "National Organ Donor Week".
