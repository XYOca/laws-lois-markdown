# Merchant Navy Veterans Day Act

> An Act to establish Merchant Navy Veterans Day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Merchant Navy Veterans Day Act*.

## Merchant Navy Veterans Day

### 2 Merchant Navy Veterans Day

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the third day of September shall be known as “Merchant Navy Veterans Day”.
