# Sir John A. Macdonald Day and the Sir Wilfrid Laurier Day Act

> An Act respecting Sir John A. Macdonald Day and Sir Wilfrid Laurier Day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Sir John A. Macdonald Day and the Sir Wilfrid Laurier Day Act*.

## Sir John A. Macdonald Day

### 2 Sir John A. Macdonald Day

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the 11th day of January shall be known under the name of "Sir John A. Macdonald Day".

## Sir Wilfrid Laurier Day

### 3 Sir Wilfrid Laurier Day

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the twentieth day of November shall be known under the name of "Sir Wilfrid Laurier Day".
