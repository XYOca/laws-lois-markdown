# Food Day in Canada Act

> An Act to establish Food Day in Canada


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Food Day in Canada Act*.

## Food Day in Canada

### 2 Food Day in Canada

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the Saturday before the first Monday in August is to be known as “Food Day in Canada”.
