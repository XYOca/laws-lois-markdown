# National Symbol of Canada Act

> An Act to provide for the recognition of the Beaver (*Castor canadensis*) as a symbol of the sovereignty of Canada


### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *National Symbol of Canada Act*.

### 2 Beaver as a symbol of Canadian sovereignty

It is hereby recognized and declared that the Beaver (*Castor canadensis*) is a symbol of the sovereignty of Canada and it is proclaimed that any representation of the Beaver (*Castor canadensis*) when used by Her Majesty in right of Canada shall be so used and so regarded.
