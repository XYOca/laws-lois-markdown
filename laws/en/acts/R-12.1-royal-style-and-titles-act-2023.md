# Royal Style and Titles Act, 2023

> An Act respecting the Royal Style and Titles, 2023


### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Royal Style and Titles Act, 2023*.

### 2 Assent to Royal Style and Titles

The Parliament of Canada assents to the issue by His Majesty of His Royal Proclamation under the Great Seal of Canada establishing for Canada the following Royal Style and Titles:

Charles the Third, by the Grace of God King of Canada and His other Realms and Territories, Head of the Commonwealth.
