# Lincoln Alexander Day Act

> An Act respecting Lincoln Alexander Day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Lincoln Alexander Day Act*.

## Lincoln Alexander Day

### 2 Lincoln Alexander Day

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the twenty-first day of January shall be known as “Lincoln Alexander Day”.

### 3 Not a legal holiday

For greater certainty, Lincoln Alexander Day is not a legal holiday or a non-juridical day.
