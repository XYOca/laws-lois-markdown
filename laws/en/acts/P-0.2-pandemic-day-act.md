# Pandemic Day Act

> An Act respecting Pandemic Observance Day


## Short Title

### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Pandemic Day Act*.

## Pandemic Observance Day

### 2 Pandemic Observance Day

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the 11th day of March is to be known as “Pandemic Observance Day”.
