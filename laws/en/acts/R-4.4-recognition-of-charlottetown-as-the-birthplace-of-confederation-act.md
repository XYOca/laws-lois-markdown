# Recognition of Charlottetown as the Birthplace of Confederation Act

> An Act to recognize Charlottetown as the birthplace of Confederation


### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *Recognition of Charlottetown as the Birthplace of Confederation Act*.

### 2 Birthplace of Confederation

Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island, is recognized as and declared to be the birthplace of Confederation.

### 3 Jurisdiction of the Minister

For greater certainty, nothing in this Act constitutes a designation within the jurisdiction of the Minister responsible for the Parks Canada Agency under the *Parks Canada Agency Act*.
