# National Anthem Act

> An Act respecting the national anthem of Canada


### 1 Short title

This Act may be cited as the *National Anthem Act*.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- 1980-81-82-83, c. 5, s. 1

</details>

### 2 National anthem

The words and music of the song “O Canada”, as set out in the schedule, are designated as the national anthem of Canada.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- 1980-81-82-83, c. 5, s. 2

</details>

### 3 Public domain

The words and music of the national anthem of Canada are hereby declared to be in the public domain.

<details>
<summary>Historical Note</summary>

- 1980-81-82-83, c. 5, s. 3

</details>
